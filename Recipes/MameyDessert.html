Mamey Dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 fruits (mamey)
  • 397 grams condensed milk

Directions

  1. Using a spoon, remove the pulp from the skin.
  2. In a blender, add the mamey and condensed milk. Puree until smooth.
  3. Add milk if too thick.
  4. Pour into dessert cups and garnish to taste.
  5. Serve cold.
